# HackBI Graphic Design Principles Workshop
> A discussion of graphic design principles focused on creating for a modern audience while also examining past design trends.

## Get Started
You don't need anything other than yourself for this workshop!

## Purpose

## Goal
Understand three of the main pillars of design and gain familiarity with past and current design trends. 

## Challenges
1. understand basic color theory
2. become familiar with dynamics
3. be introduced to spacing
4. examine different eras of design
